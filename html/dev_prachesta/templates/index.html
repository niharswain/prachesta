Welcome to Prachesta

A nonprofit organization dedicated to making a positive impact in the lives of those in need. With a passion for change and compassion at our core, we strive to address pressing social issues and create a better world for all. Through our collaborative efforts and generous support, we work tirelessly to provide essential resources, empower communities, and promote sustainable development. Our programs encompass areas such as education, healthcare, poverty alleviation, and environmental conservation.

With the help of our dedicated volunteers and donors, we have successfully touched the lives of countless individuals and families, offering them hope and a chance for a brighter future. Join us today in our mission to transform lives, uplift communities, and foster lasting change. Together, we can make a difference and build a more equitable and compassionate society.

Our Mission

Our mission at Prachesta is to empower individuals and communities by addressing their most pressing needs and creating sustainable solutions. We strive to foster positive social change and improve the lives of those facing adversity. Through our dedicated efforts, we aim to provide access to education, healthcare, and essential resources, while promoting equality, justice, and human rights. We believe in the power of compassion, collaboration, and innovation to create lasting impact. Our mission is to inspire hope, ignite potential, and transform lives, one person at a time. Together, with the support of our donors, volunteers, and partners, we are committed to building a more inclusive and equitable world for all

Our Vision

At Prachesta, our vision is to envision a world where every individual, regardless of their circumstances, has the opportunity to thrive and lead a fulfilling life. We strive to create a society where compassion, kindness, and generosity prevail, and where the most vulnerable members are supported and empowered. Through our nonprofit organization, we aspire to be a catalyst for positive change, addressing critical social issues and providing vital resources and support to those in need. By fostering collaboration, innovation, and community engagement, we envision a future where our collective efforts create lasting impact, inspire hope, and transform lives. Together, we can create a more equitable and compassionate world for generations to come.

About Us

Prachesta Foundation plants saplings and supports them to become trees. At Prachesta Foundation we have been creating urban and rural forests , support farmers with fruit trees, rejuvenate lakes and water bodies and install solar lamps in villages.

Prachesta Foundation does all this with support from individuals and corporates which wish to bring change to the society under their CSR (Corporate Social Responsibility) initiatives. Prachesta Foundation is an NGO in India registered under ssection 12A and 80G of Income Tax.
