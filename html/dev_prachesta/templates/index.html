About Our Charity

Prachesta Foundation plants saplings and supports them to become trees. At Prachesta Foundation we have been creating urban and rural forests , support farmers with fruit trees, rejuvenate lakes and water bodies and install solar lamps in villages.

Prachesta Foundation does all this with support from individuals and corporates which wish to bring change to the society under their CSR (Corporate Social Responsibility) initiatives. Prachesta Foundation is an NGO in India registered under ssection 12A and 80G of Income Tax.

Our Mission

Prachesta Foundation, a non-profit organization in india is to empower underprivileged children, youth and women through relevant education,healthcare. At Prachesta we are focucsed to create urban and rural forests and help farmers in innovative technology and methodology.

Our Vision

To educate and empower underprivileged children and support them till they are gainfully employed. To do our best so that the current generation of underprivileged children living below the poverty line integrate seamlessly into mainstream society. To impart quality education, and all-round development, including skill building, in order to provide avenues for employment that are suited to every child’s dominant intelligence and strengths. To help every needy who needs medical facility. To stop the climate change and bringing back the greenery to our lives.
