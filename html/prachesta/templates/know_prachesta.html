Know Your Organization

Prachesta Foundation plants saplings and supports them to become trees. At Prachesta Foundation we have been creating urban and rural forests , support farmers with fruit trees, rejuvenate lakes and water bodies and install solar lamps in villages.

Prachesta Foundation does all this with support from individuals and corporates which wish to bring change to the society under their CSR (Corporate Social Responsibility) initiatives. Prachesta Foundation is an NGO in India registered under ssection 12A and 80G of Income Tax.
